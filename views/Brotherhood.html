Our Mission

Brotherhood

We have numerous ways to ensure that each brother gets to fully enjoy their college experience. We have events year-round, including but not limited to: semi-formal and formal socials, as well as other retreats to allow brothers to bond with each other.

Academics

Our chapter requires each member to obtain a GPA of 3.0 or higher. We provide a number of resources to allow our brothers to succeed academically. Brothers attend weekly study tables where they can get help on their school work, as well as receive advice from a host of older brothers from a variety of backgrounds. The Sigma Chapter also boasts a large and diverse network of alumni who are available to provide career counseling and guidance.

Philanthropy

Theta Xi's national charity is the National Multiple Sclerosis Society. Our chapter holds an annual "Defend the Diag" to help raise awareness and collect money for the cause. We also hold a number of smaller philanthropic events throughout the semester.

Social

We host events to allow our brothers to interact with other members of the greek community.
